ABOUT US

Academic and Career Council, is a student body dedicated to improve the academic system and culture of the institute by working on various policies and conducting sessions to disseminate information.

EVENTS

CAREER CONNECT

Career Connect is an annual flagship event under the Academics and Career Council. The primary intent behind this event is to encourage students to explore and equip them better to make informed and wise choices in their professional, social and personal endeavour.

INSTITUTE RESEARCH SYMPOSIUM

Intitute Research Symposium(IRS) is an annual event in which we intend to provide a platform for students to showcase their research to the larger community. Our goal is to provide a space for students, faculty and members of the scientific community to share their cutting-edge innovations with one another.

National Student's Research Convention(NSRC)

NSRC is a flagship event that serves as a platform for the brightest minds in academia and industry to showcase their cutting-edge research and innovation ideas. It also provides opportunities for students, especially those who showcase their research and innovative work, to gain insight about their field of interest through talks and interactions with industry leaders as well as the faculty.

INITIATIVES

UG/PG Academics

The UG/PG Academics Wing takes up the responsibility of the academics related work of the Academic and Career Council. It acts as an interface between the institute policy makers and the students on matters related to academics.

Research

The Research Wing of the council aims at cultivating and promoting research interests in the student community, by providing the freedom and opportunities to work upon diverse areas of research.

International Relations

The International Relations Wing works in close association with the Office of International Relations (OIR), IIT Kanpur to improve collaborations of the Institute and its foreign counterparts.

Career Development

The Career Development Wing of the Council, with the mandate of Career Development of students, shall provide an extraordinary boost in regard to Career Aspects.
